Research Interests

Reasoning across views, scenes, and actions.

I am interested in building and evaluating models that understand spatial relations, visual evidence, and physical-world structure.

Multimodal Spatial Reasoning

Diagnosing how multimodal models reason about relative position, orientation, viewpoint changes, and scene layout.

Cross-view Visual Understanding

Connecting remote sensing, street-view, and other viewpoints for robust spatial understanding.

Visual & Physical Reasoning

Exploring how models infer object relations, affordances, causality, and physical constraints from vision.

Embodied AI Evaluation

Extending spatial reasoning benchmarks toward embodied agents, navigation, and vision-language-action systems.

What I am building

CVSBench

A benchmark for cross-view spatial reasoning and visual imagination. It studies whether multimodal models can infer spatial relations across remote sensing and street-view images, and whether generated visual information can improve reasoning.
