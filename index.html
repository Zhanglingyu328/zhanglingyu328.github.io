Greetings! I am currently an undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science and Technology at Xi'an Jiaotong University. I am conducting undergraduate research in Prof. Xiangyong Cao's group.

My current research focuses on multimodal spatial reasoning, cross-view visual understanding, and visual imagination for remote sensing and street-view imagery. I am especially interested in evaluating whether multimodal models can understand spatial relations across different viewpoints.

My broader research interests include Computer Vision, Multimodal Large Language Models, Visual Reasoning, Physical Reasoning, and Embodied AI.
